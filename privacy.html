Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the MangaShelf app (herein referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Sok4h (herein referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects and uses certain information to provide a better user experience and improve service quality. The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

The Application utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase — Used to monitor and analyze the use of the Application.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Used to identify, track, and fix software bugs and crashes.

2. Data Types Collected

  • Device Identifiers — Such as Google Advertising ID or unique device tokens.
  • Usage Data — Information on how you interact with the app (e.g., screens visited, buttons clicked).
  • Diagnostics — Crash logs and performance reports.

3. Data Security

The Service Provider is committed to safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. All data transmitted by the Application is encrypted in transit using HTTPS protocols.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since the Application stores your manga collection locally on your device (using Room/Local Storage), you can delete all your personal collection data simply by clearing the Application cache or uninstalling the app.

For any data collected by third-party services such as Analytics, you may contact the Service Provider directly.
